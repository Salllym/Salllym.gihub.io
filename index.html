About me

Hello! I'm a forever curious individual with a designer mindset that's grounded by an analytical and research perspective who strives to create meaningful, intuitive, and accessible experiences for people. I draw inspirations from my diverse experiences and everyday observations.


I'm also detail-oriented with the ability to analyze large sets of data and enjoy leveraging my background and skill set to support detailed and efficient analysis. Overall, I find great satisfaction in extracting insights from data that inform decision-making and drive growth.


I graduated from the University of Illinois at Chicago with a degree in Health Information Management and earned certificates in UX Design and Data Science and Visualization from Springboard and Northwestern University.


My strengths in analytical problem-solving and cross-functional team collaborations combined with a passion to lean into the data make for an advantageous asset to any organization.
